Project proof

Proof in real projects

The strongest proof is shown through real project outcomes — difficult quality problems made visible, structured and improved.

Case 01 · Recall reversal

$1M field exposure reversed through process redesign — not more inspection

The failure revealed the supplier is masking the material instability through over-processing. The specification had no adhesion standard — no legal recourse. A clean-sheet process analysis changed the failure path entirely. Annual scrap went from $600,000 to $1,500. Lead time from 12 weeks to 5 days. Part cost down 75%.

Case 02 · Ramp-up stability

100 units per year to 1,500 per month — 18,000% demand increase in 12 months

A single marketing event created overnight global demand for a craftsman-built product. The internal architecture had to be re-engineered behind an unchanged external design, under full regulatory compliance. Result: full volume on schedule, 30% lower per-piece cost, zero quality failures in the first high-volume batch.

Common patterns

Where risks and quality problems usually hide​

A part can be formally correct and still fail in assembly, if the specification, tolerance, material behavior or use case was misunderstood. We help you avoiding these issues.

Recurring defects often mean the symptom was corrected, but the interface that created the failure was not changed.

But reality looks different! We help you reverse-engineering a process that meets the standard and reality at the same time.​

If critical know-how sits with one person, the company is exposed during growth, absence, retirement or handover.
