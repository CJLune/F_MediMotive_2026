# MediMotive — Shop-Floor Crisis Explainer Video
## Production Prompt (Corrected & Final)

---

## PURPOSE

This is the production-ready prompt for the MediMotive explainer video titled:
**"How one upstream decision becomes a shop-floor crisis"**

Use this prompt with AI video tools (HeyGen, Pictory, Runway, Synthesia) or hand it directly to a video editor or motion designer. It corrects all errors from the original draft and aligns every word with MediMotive's brand voice.

---

## BRAND VOICE RULES (Do Not Break These)

- Tone: practical, direct, calm, technically credible, human
- No buzzwords, no consultant theatre, no corporate transformation language
- Speak like a senior quality engineer who has been on the shop floor — not like a marketing agency
- Use: "shop-floor reality," "real process," "practical action," "upstream cause," "purchasing context"
- Avoid: "world-class," "optimize," "agile," "digital transformation," "one-stop-shop"

---

## VIDEO SPECS

- Duration: 60–90 seconds
- Format: 16:9 widescreen (1920×1080 minimum)
- Style: Animated infographic explainer — clean motion design over the existing illustrated flow graphic
- Background: Deep navy (#1c2e4a) consistent with MediMotive brand
- Accent colors: Orange (#f97316) for the crisis/breakdown beat; teal (#486966) for resolution beats
- Music: Neutral, understated background — no dramatic builds, no corporate uplift tracks
- Narration: Professional male voice, calm and measured, mid-tempo — NOT sales energy
