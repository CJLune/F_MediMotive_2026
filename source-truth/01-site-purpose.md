# Site Purpose

## Purpose

MediMotive is a professional credibility and evidence website.

It should show:

- what MediMotive stands for
- how MediMotive thinks
- what practical manufacturing experience sits behind it
- why Björn Seiler is credible
- what proof exists through case studies, credentials, and work-context visuals

## Current Role

The current website is not an aggressive consulting funnel.

It should support:

- employer trust
- industry credibility
- professional visitor confidence
- future business conversations
- evidence of experience and working philosophy

## Positioning Anchor

MediMotive stands for practical quality work close to production.

It connects shop-floor reality, supplier communication, regulatory expectations, and management decisions so quality problems can be understood where they actually happen — not only where they are documented.

## Audience

The website speaks to:

- manufacturing SMEs
- machining and assembly companies
- employers and professional visitors
- industry contacts in quality, production, supplier management, QMS, and regulatory environments
- companies or contacts evaluating quality, supplier escalation, process stability, regulatory, and transition-security capability

## Non-Goals

The website should not be:

- Björn’s CV only
- a sales-heavy consulting funnel
- a certificate dump
- a casual gallery
- a generic quality consulting site
- a paper-consulting website
