# Constraints

## Brand / Logo

- Do not create a fake logo.
- Do not create an “MM” icon.
- Use text-only MediMotive wordmark.
- MediMotive wordmark links to `index.html`.

## Header Navigation

Use compact main navigation only:

- Expertise → `services.html`
- Case Studies → `case-studies.html`
- About → `about.html`
- Contact → `contact.html`

Do not add to the main header:

- Home
- Gallery
- Method
- Regions
- Email CTA
- Shop-Floor Check CTA

## Forms / CTAs

- Do not add a contact form unless explicitly requested.
- Do not add a header email CTA.
- Contact should be simple and professional.
- Current site should not push aggressive booking language.

## Color Palette

Approved palette:

- `#1c2e4a`
- `#486966`
- `#027343`
- `#f0fdf4`
- `#f8fafc`

Do not change the brand palette unless explicitly instructed.

## About Page

- About must be MediMotive-first and Björn-supported.
- Do not make About a personal CV.
- Do not list all certificates on About.
- Use credential groups on About and link to `certificates.html`.

## Certificates

- `certificates.html` is the full credential archive.
- Certificate images belong primarily on `certificates.html`.
- About can summarize credentials and link to the archive.

## Gallery / Visual Proof

- Do not add Gallery to the main nav.
- `gallery.html` can exist as a visual proof hub.
- Link visual proof from About and footer.
- Visual proof must feel professional, not casual or Instagram-like.

## Images

- Do not show empty placeholders.
- Do not use stock photos.
- Do not show confidential customer names, logos, workers, drawings, serial numbers, documents, or production details without permission.

## Training / Legal Boundary

Do not position MediMotive as a direct classroom-style training provider.

Correct framing:

MediMotive identifies knowledge gaps, creates technical materials, supports the selection of suitable external training partners, and verifies whether training has been effective in real process behavior.
