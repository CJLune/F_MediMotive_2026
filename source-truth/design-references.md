# Design references (2026 homepage redesign)

| Reference | What we borrow | What we avoid |
|-----------|----------------|---------------|
| [McKinsey](https://www.mckinsey.com/) | Flat hero canvas, headline-first stack, editorial whitespace, de-emphasised meta | Stock video heroes, dense nav mega-menus, decorative gradients |
| [BCG](https://www.bcg.com/) | Calm scroll reveals, staggered section entry, active chapter state, restrained transitions | Parallax, Lottie, auto-playing carousels, page loaders |
| [Arup Projects](https://www.arup.com/en-us/projects/) | Featured project row + editorial grid, large imagery with title/context under photo | Uniform 3-column card grids with heavy chrome |

**MediMotive constraints:** approved palette only; footer design frozen; no copy changes during layout passes; English master until German pages exist.
